Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RoundPenny

How does round-up investing work?▾
Every time a user makes a purchase, RoundPenny rounds up the transaction to the nearest increment and invests the spare change automatically. For example, a $4.50 coffee becomes $5.00, and $0.50 goes into the user's investment portfolio.
What does white-label mean?▾
White-label means you can offer RoundPenny's investment platform under your own brand. We provide the infrastructure, APIs, and compliance framework. Your customers see your brand, not ours.
Is the platform compliant with regulations?▾
Yes. RoundPenny is built with compliance in mind — SOC 2 Type II audit-ready, GDPR compliant, CCPA compliant, and built to meet FINRA/RIA requirements for the US market. Bank-grade encryption (AES-256) and full audit trails.
How do I integrate RoundPenny?▾
Integrate via our REST API with webhooks for real-time events. SDKs for Go, JavaScript, and Python. Typical integration takes 2-4 weeks. Contact our partnerships team for API documentation and sandbox access.
What infrastructure do I need?▾
None. RoundPenny is fully managed. We handle all infrastructure — 15 microservices, Kafka, PostgreSQL, Redis, monitoring, and security. You focus on your product, we handle the investment backend.
