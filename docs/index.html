About Us

RoundPenny is an open-source micro-investment platform that turns the spare change from your everyday purchases into investments. Built with 15 microservices, Kafka, PostgreSQL, and Kubernetes — enterprise-grade infrastructure from day one.

🎯

Our Purpose

Make financial literacy and investment habits accessible to everyone. Show that even small savings can grow. With our white-label API, any platform can offer its own investment product — turning unnoticed spare change into meaningful financial futures.

👁️

Vision & Mission

  • Vision: Build a world where every person's pocket change becomes an investment vehicle — a global ecosystem where financial inclusion knows no borders.
  • Mission: Empower fintechs, neobanks, and HCM platforms to launch investment products in minutes using our open-source, API-first infrastructure. Protect user assets with automatic round-ups, real-time reconciliation, and enterprise-grade security standards.
  • Values: Transparency, security, scalability, community-driven development, financial inclusion.
