## 6. Round-Up Service

### 6.1 How Rounding Works

When you link a funding source (bank account, debit card, or credit card) and authorize round-up transactions, RoundPenny will:

1. Calculate the spare change difference between each purchase amount and the nearest whole dollar;
2. Aggregate round-up amounts according to your selected settlement schedule;
3. Transfer the aggregated amount from your linked funding source to your RoundPenny investment account.

### 6.2 Settlement Schedule

- **Daily Settlement:** Round-ups are aggregated and settled at the end of each business day.
- **Weekly Settlement:** Round-ups are aggregated weekly and settled on the first business day following the end of each calendar week.

You may change your settlement preference at any time via your account settings.

### 6.3 Minimum Round-Up Threshold

RoundPenny reserves the right to set a minimum aggregate round-up threshold before settlement is initiated. Any un-settled round-ups below this threshold will carry forward to the next settlement period.

### 6.4 Reversals and Chargebacks

If a purchase transaction is reversed, charged back, or refunded, the associated round-up amount will be reversed from your investment account. If insufficient funds are available, the reversal may result in a negative balance.
