# RoundPenny — LinkedIn Outreach Strategy

## Targeting
- **Investors:** Fintech-focused VCs (Seed/Series A), Angel investors in fintech
- **Partners:** Neobanks, HCM/Payroll platforms, traditional banks
- **Connectors:** Fintech consultants, industry analysts
